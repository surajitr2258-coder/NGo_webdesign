Our Story

Reaching Those Who Are Most Difficult to Reach

From the very beginning, Anubhav has focused on reaching those who are most difficult to reach—not because their needs are smaller, but because their locations make support harder to deliver.

Where We Work

We work primarily in remote and under-served regions of West Bengal, with special attention to areas such as the Sundarbans and Namkhana, and the forest and rural belts of Jhargram, Bankura, and Purulia. In these regions, families often face limited access to essential services due to challenging terrain, distance from urban centers, and gaps in regular aid and outreach.

Our Beginning

Our first major effort was grounded in direct community service. In our initial phase, we supported nearly 100 households across Sundarbans and Namkhana, delivering help where it was most needed and building relationships based on trust, dignity, and continued presence. This experience shaped our core belief: meaningful change begins when support reaches people not only in times of crisis, but also in the long journey of recovery and resilience.

Our Approach

At Anubhav, we believe in action backed by empathy and responsibility. We aim to work closely with local communities, understand real needs on the ground, and respond with practical, respectful, and timely support. Whether it is in riverine islands vulnerable to climate shocks or villages in forested regions with limited connectivity, we remain committed to being there—consistently and sincerely.

Today & Tomorrow

Today, Anubhav continues to operate across different parts of West Bengal, strengthening its efforts to serve people in regions where assistance is often scarce. With the collective energy of volunteers, well-wishers, and community partners, we strive to bring hope, support, and opportunity to families who deserve to be seen, heard, and supported.

Anubhav is not just an organization—
it is an experience of coming together for humanity.
